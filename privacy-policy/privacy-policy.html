1. About This App

ToDo App is a standalone application that runs locally on the user’s device. It does not require user registration, accounts, or login to access its features.

The app is provided by BitPull Labs.

2. Personal Data & Privacy (EU GDPR)

ToDoApp does not collect, process, store or transmit any personal data, as defined under Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (General Data Protection Regulation - GDPR).

  • All data created by the user remains exlusively on the user's device.
  • No data is shared with the developer or third parties.
  • No analytics, tracking, profiling or advertising systems are used.

As no personal data is processed, GDPR user rights (such as access, rectification or erasure) are not applicable to this app.

3. Permissions

The app does not request access to:

The app functions fully offline and does not require an internet connection.
No data is transmitted outside the user's device.
